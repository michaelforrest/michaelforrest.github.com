Here is something I started a couple of years ago when dubstep still seemed quite cool.

I was searching for the secret to making those types of musical progressions and came up with this app for interactively toggling and tuning tempo-locked loops. The visual aspect was important to me - I wanted to make sure there was always accurate graphical feedback tightly synchronised to the sound a la Teenage Engineering OP-1.
